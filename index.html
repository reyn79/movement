movement martial arts

Movement Martial Arts is a martial arts gym and club based in Sydney which provides classes and coaching specialising in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu (BJJ), Muay Thai Kickboxing, and Mixed Martial Arts (MMA). We offer classes and training to suit ages 6+, for people of all sizes and skill levels.

3 best friends were brought together by a shared passion for martial arts and wanted to share this with others, and so Movement Martial Arts was born. Our mission is to create a community where everyone has the chance to achieve their goals and reach their full potential.

We focus on 3 Cs:

Coaching

We provide world class coaching and invest in developing our students to their full potential.

Culture

We drive a culture that makes everyone feel welcome, valued and supported. Most of all we want our students to have fun when they come train with us!

Community

Ultimately we are creating a community of people with a shared passion and love for their sport.

Our Styles

Muay Thai Kickboxing

Muay Thai is a style of kickboxing that originates from Thailand and uses the whole body. It is referred to as the art of 8 limbs, incorporating hands, elbows, feet, knees and grappling into a comprehensive and effective striking based martial art.

Muay Thai is a great cardiovascular workout, effective for self-defence and is a dominant striking style to train for competitive kickboxing and MMA. Our kickboxing classes aim to teach you effective fighting techniques and will provide you with a challenging workout.

Brazilian Jiu Jitsu (BJJ)

BJJ is a combat sport and a self-defence system that focuses on grappling and ground fighting, emphasising position, control and submissions such as chokes, arm locks and leg locks.

Our head coaches have many years of experience in both coaching and competition, so come join our BJJ classes!

Mixed Martial Arts (MMA)

MMA is the world's fastest growing sport. It is an extremely comprehensive fighting style, combining elements of boxing, kickboxing, jiu jitsu, wrestling and many other martial arts.

Our MMA training program requires base experience in other areas of martial arts, and are for students interested in competition and a career in fighting. Our program will teach you both technique and strategy, with support from our dedicated coaches, who will hone your abilities and enable you to reach your potential.

Kids and Teens (Jiu Jitsu Ages 6+, Muay Thai Kickboxing Ages 14+)

Our kids classes help to build our students both physically and mentally. It can be tough for kids and teens to build healthy self-esteem and confidence in themselves. Here at Movement Martial Arts our kids BJJ and Muay Thai classes are not only about learning technique in a fun sport, but also a great way to build discipline, confidence, self-esteem and self-defence in a safe and welcoming environment.

We cater to new beginners right through to world class competitors for both kids and teens.
